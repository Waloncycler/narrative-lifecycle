# 01 Theory: 名、资、定价、实、势、阻力

## 名 / Perception

Market attention, interpretation, label clarity, keyword convergence, and shared narrative language.

## 资 / Capital Confirmation

Whether capital tests the narrative through leader stocks, breadth, volume, pullback resilience, and persistence.

## 定价 / Pricing Adoption

Whether the narrative becomes an institutional pricing and allocation language: valuation reframing, financial translation, core assets, trackable data.

## 实 / Reality Validation

Hard evidence: orders, revenue, customers, regulatory approval, clinical data, product delivery, profit contribution, repeat purchase.

## 势 / Feedback Momentum

Whether Perception → Capital → Reality → Perception forms a self-reinforcing loop.

## 阻力 / Friction

Execution friction: technical, commercial, regulatory, mapping, evidence gaps.

Valuation friction: valuation crowding, expectation saturation, good-news fatigue, weak pullback support.

## 数据置信度 / Data Confidence

Whether evidence is broad, authoritative, recent, balanced, and covers all required layers.
