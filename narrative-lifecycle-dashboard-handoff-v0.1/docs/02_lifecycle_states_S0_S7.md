# 02 Lifecycle States S0-S7

## Short Definitions

- **S0 = 信息存在，但市场未看见。**
- **S1 = 市场看见，但尚未解释。**
- **S2 = 市场解释，但标签未稳定。**
- **S3 = 标签稳定，但资金未充分测试。**
- **S4 = 资金测试，但尚未进入定价采纳。**
- **S5 = 定价采纳，但现实尚未充分验证。**
- **S6 = 现实验证，但系统演化尚未分岔完成。**
- **S7 = 验证之后进入主流化、透支或再叙事化。**

## S0 Latent Signal

Weak real-world signal exists, such as research, patent, prototype, early financing, or policy pre-study. Market attention is absent or limited.

## S1 Attention

The market sees an event, but still asks: what happened? The explanation of future significance is not stable.

## S2 Narrative Hypothesis

The market starts explaining the event. Multiple hypotheses compete. Future extrapolation appears, but labels are not yet stable.

## S3 Labeling

A stable label forms. The market can name the theme and map initial beneficiaries, but capital has not fully validated it.

## S4 Consensus Testing

Capital starts testing the label through price, volume, leaders, and sector breadth. It is still a trading consensus, not yet pricing adoption.

## S5 Pricing Adoption

The narrative enters valuation, financial translation, institutional modeling, and allocation language.

## S6 Reality Validation

Reality starts answering market expectations through hard evidence. S6 is not automatically safe because expectations may already be high.

## S7 Branches

- **S7A Mainstream**: sustained validation and long-term allocation.
- **S7B Exhaustion**: expectation saturation, good-news fatigue, reality below priced expectations.
- **S7C Mutation**: a mature narrative generates new early-stage branches.
